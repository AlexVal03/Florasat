üìñ Technical Glossary

NDVI
Normalized Difference Vegetation Index. Measures the amount and health of vegetation.
Phenology
Study of periodic biological cycles of plants and their relationship with climate.
Superbloom
Massive and synchronized bloom event visible from space.
MODIS
Moderate Resolution Imaging Spectroradiometer, key NASA instrument.
Landsat
Longest-running Earth observation satellite program, operational since 1972.
EVI
Enhanced Vegetation Index. Optimized for areas with high biomass.
Spectral Resolution
Sensor's ability to distinguish different light wavelengths.
Pollinator
Organism that transfers pollen between flowers, essential for plant reproduction.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do satellites detect plant blooming? ‚ñº
Satellites use sensors that capture light in different wavelengths. Flowers reflect light differently than leaves, allowing detection of spectral changes. Indices like NDVI measure vegetation health and density, and sudden changes can indicate bloom events.
What is a superbloom and why is it important? ‚ñº
A superbloom is a massive flowering event that occurs when exceptional weather conditions (such as abundant rains after droughts) cause millions of wildflowers to bloom simultaneously. They are important because they indicate significant environmental changes and are visible from space.
How can I contribute to Florasat? ‚ñº
You can use the GLOBE Observer app to report local bloom observations. This data helps validate satellite detections and improves the accuracy of our predictive models.
What satellites does Florasat use? ‚ñº
We use data from multiple NASA missions: Landsat 8/9 (30m resolution), MODIS Terra/Aqua (250-500m resolution), VIIRS Suomi NPP, and occasionally ESA's Sentinel-2. Each satellite provides complementary information.
How does climate change affect bloom patterns? ‚ñº
Climate change is altering bloom cycles: some species bloom earlier, others later, and some change their duration. This can desynchronize plant-pollinator relationships and affect entire ecosystems. Florasat helps monitor these changes.
